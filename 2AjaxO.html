Cinematographer Productions

Cinematographer production is an event promoting foundation in Poland, particularly in Silesia region. Apart of promoting them, they are organizing them from time to time.
I have created a logo and designed the website. Then I have written a lightweight CMS based on Kohana to manage this website. Also I have created buisness cards in Illustrator for members and I have prepared them to print.

Technologies:

Adobe Illustrator, MySQL, PHP5, Kohana 3.3.1, Adobe Photoshop, Javascript, jQuery, HTML5, CSS3
